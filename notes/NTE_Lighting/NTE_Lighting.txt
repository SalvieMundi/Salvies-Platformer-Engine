Lighting Variables

lighting = holds all information regarding the object's lighting engine properties
	isEmitter = boolean, whether this object emits light
	distance = real, how far the light stretches in pixels
	strength = real, how much the light penetrates the ambient darkness (0 to 1, or 0 to 100)
	clr = the color the light emits (c_white means no color)
	isCaster = boolean, whether this object casts shadows
